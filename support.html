Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the recording cylinder?

To change the recording cylinder, lift the cover, remove the old cylinder, and carefully insert the new one. Make sure it is properly aligned before closing the cover.

How do I clean and maintain my phonograph?

Regularly dust your phonograph with a soft cloth and use a gentle cleaner for the horn. Avoid using water or harsh chemicals. Check the manual for detailed maintenance tips.

Can I use modern recording media with my phonograph?

While the Edison Phonograph is designed for wax cylinders, adapters are available for using modern media. Contact support for more information on compatible accessories.

How do I troubleshoot playback issues?

If you experience playback issues, check the alignment of the recording cylinder and the condition of the needle. Refer to the troubleshooting guide in your manual or contact support for further assistance.

What warranty options are available?

We offer a standard one-year warranty on all new phonographs, with options to extend up to three years. Visit our warranty page for more details and to register your product.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@phonographinc.com or call us at 1-800-EDISON. Our support hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM EST.

Are there any tutorials available for using the phonograph?

Yes, we have a range of tutorials and videos available on our website. Visit our tutorials page to learn more about using and maintaining your phonograph.
